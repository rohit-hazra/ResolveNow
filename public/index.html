Tracking & Visibility

End-to-End Complaint Resolution Workflow

1. Student Registers Complaint

The student logs in and submits a complaint with relevant category, priority and details.

2. Coordinator Reviews Case

The coordinator monitors complaints and updates the handling status according to progress.

3. Complaint is Processed

The complaint can remain pending, become assigned, move into progress, or be reverted for correction.

4. Final Resolution

The issue is resolved successfully or closed through a withdrawal action where applicable.

Designed Around Two Main Users

ResolveNow is designed specifically for students and coordinators, so each user gets the right tools and responsibilities inside the system.

Student Panel

Students can register complaints, view all their submitted complaints, search and filter their records, edit pending or reverted complaints, and withdraw complaints when allowed by the system.

Coordinator Panel

Coordinators can access complaint records, review student submissions, and update complaint progress for institutional monitoring and redressal handling.

Core Features for Daily Complaint Management

Category-Based Reporting

Supports complaint categories such as plumbing, electrical, internet, housekeeping, mess, security and administration.

Priority Handling

Each complaint can be marked with low, medium or high priority for better issue management.

Complaint History

Students can review their complaint records and monitor status updates from submission to closure.

Editable Requests

Pending and reverted complaints can be edited by students when changes are needed before final processing.

Search, Filter & Sort

Students can quickly find complaints using search, filter and sort options for better tracking.

Secure Access Flow

Separate login and dashboard access for students and coordinators keeps the system organized and secure.
